FAQ

Travel Questions
About Iraq

When is the best time to visit Iraq?

Most travelers prefer October to April for comfortable weather. Summer can be extremely hot, especially in central and southern Iraq.

Do I need a visa to enter Iraq?

Visa rules depend on your nationality. Always check the latest official requirements before traveling. If you contact us, tell us your passport country and travel dates.

Is it safe to travel in Iraq?

Safety can vary by city and situation. Use common sense, stay updated with reliable advisories, and avoid sensitive areas. For first-time visitors, guided planning is recommended.

How do I move around inside cities?

Most travelers use taxis or local drivers. In some areas you can also use ride-hailing apps. For multi-stop days, a driver is often the easiest option.

What should I wear and how should I behave respectfully?

Modest clothing is recommended, especially in religious cities and shrines. Be respectful with photography, ask before taking photos of people, and follow local customs.
